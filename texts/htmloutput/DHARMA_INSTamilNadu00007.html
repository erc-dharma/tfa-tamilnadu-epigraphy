Translation by G. Vijayavenugopal

1 Prosperity! Fortune!

1-2 Sacred service tiruppaṇi of Aḻakiya-Nampirāṭṭi, wife of the Kāṭavarāyar alias Vīṟamōhaṉ [...]

Commentary

Vīramohaṉ Kāṭavarāyaṉ may be Ēḻicaimohaṉ, the son of the Kāṭavarāya King Āṭkolliyār.

Since this inscription is found on the base of a small shrine, it may refer to the construction of this small shrine, as a service.
