    Translation by Emmanuel Francis

    1 Prosperity! Fortune!

    1-3 30th year of the glorious king Rājādhirāja, the lord.

    3-12 [There are] 50 gold coins...

    12-21 ...

    21-28 ...

    28-30 ...

    30-31 ...

    31-38 ...

    38-45 ...
